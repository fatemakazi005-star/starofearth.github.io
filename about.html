About Event Horizon

Bringing the universe's most extreme physics to everyone.

Our Mission

Science for
Every Mind

Event Horizon was built on a simple belief: the most extreme and awe-inspiring phenomena in the universe should be accessible to everyone — not just physicists and astronomers.

We translate cutting-edge astrophysics into visuals, interactive experiences, and plain language. No prior knowledge needed. Just curiosity.

From the anatomy of a singularity to the history of gravitational wave detection, every page is crafted to spark wonder and deepen understanding.

What We Stand For

Our Values

🌌
Accuracy First

Every claim is sourced from peer-reviewed research or verified by our scientific team. We never sacrifice correctness for drama.

🔓
Openly Accessible

Science belongs to everyone. This site is free, open-source on GitHub, and designed to be usable without any prior astrophysics knowledge.

✨
Beauty Matters

Great design and great science aren't opposites. We believe an interface that inspires awe makes the content more memorable and meaningful.

🛰️
Always Updating

Astrophysics moves fast. We update this site as new discoveries are made — from gravitational wave events to new black hole images.
